🔬 About DeepPhaseLab / 关于深相实验室

DeepPhaseLab is an open modeling platform focused on microstructural evolution in energy materials. We integrate phase-field modeling with multi-physics coupling to simulate diffusion, reaction, stress, and structural transformation across systems such as SOFCs, lithium-ion, and solid-state batteries.

深相实验室是一个专注于能源材料微结构演化模拟的建模平台，基于相场法融合扩散、应力和反应等多物理场机制，广泛适用于燃料电池、锂电池、固态电池等系统的数字化仿真与预测分析。
