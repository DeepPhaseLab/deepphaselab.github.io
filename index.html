Welcome to DeepPhaseLab

Microstructure simulation platform for energy materials.

DeepPhaseLab is a lightweight, scalable simulation platform focused on phase-field modeling of microstructure evolution in batteries, fuel cells, and advanced materials. Our tool integrates reaction-diffusion, stress coupling, and morphological evolution under redox conditions.
